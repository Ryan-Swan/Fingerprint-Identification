User guide - Registration

Student card support

The system is built to support the previous card authentication. Simply scan your student card to either be authenticated or be prompted to sign up for biometric methods.

Registering fingerprint

To register fingerprint, first scan your student card.

Then select Register fingerprint

Finally place your finger on the fingerprint scanner. If fingerprint is valid and saved, the following screen is shown

Registering face

To register face, first scan your student card.

Then select Register face

Finally, look at the webcam until the countdown ends.

If face is valid and saved, the following screen is shown
